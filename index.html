01 / HOW IT WORKS

MICRO-ENCAPSULATED FUSION MATRIX

Creatine is protected inside a multi-layer capsule engineered to resist heat and moisture, then suspended in a coffee-neutral carrier. This preserves creatine integrity while keeping texture and taste unchanged.
