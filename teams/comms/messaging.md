# Key Messaging — FUSE Creatine

## Core Message

**FUSE makes creatine supplementation effortless by dissolving instantly in your daily coffee.**

## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

"FUSE is Britain's first coffee-optimised creatine. We've micro-encapsulated creatine monohydrate so it dissolves in your morning coffee in under 3 seconds — no grit, no stirring, no taste change. It's the easiest way to get the world's most studied sports supplement into your daily routine."

## Language Rules

### Always Use
- "Coffee-optimised" (not "coffee-flavoured")
- "Micro-encapsulated" (when discussing technology)
- "Creatine monohydrate" (specific form)
- "Science-backed" or "peer-reviewed" (credibility)

### Never Use
- "Miracle" or "magic" (hype language)
- "Guaranteed results" (unsubstantiated)
- "Burns fat" or "builds muscle" (unapproved claims)
- "Bro" or gym slang (not our audience)
- Any claim not in `teams/legal/claims-register.md`

## Crisis Messages

### Product Safety Concern
*Template*: "At FUSE, product safety is our absolute priority. We are investigating [issue] and will provide a full update within [timeframe]. In the meantime, [action for customers]."

### Negative Press
*Template*: "We appreciate the coverage and would welcome the opportunity to share the science behind FUSE Creatine. [Offer spokesperson / data]."

*Escalate all crisis situations to CEO immediately.*
