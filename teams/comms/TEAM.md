# Comms Team — FUSE Creatine

## Identity

We manage how FUSE Creatine speaks to the world. We own public relations, social media presence, community building, and brand voice.

## Team Code: COM

## Capabilities

- Public relations and media outreach
- Social media strategy and management
- Community building
- Crisis communications
- Brand voice and messaging
- Spokesperson preparation

## Key Responsibilities

1. Define and maintain brand voice
2. Manage social media presence
3. Handle media relations
4. Build community
5. Prepare crisis communications playbook

## Dependencies

- Reads: `docs/brand-voice.md` — tone and personality
- Reads: `teams/legal/claims-register.md` — approved statements
- Provides: Messaging guidance to Marketing, Digital Content, Sales

## Communication

All public statements must align with brand voice.
Crisis situations require immediate escalation to CEO.
Social media responses follow the messaging matrix.
Coordinate with Digital Content on content distribution.
