# Product Team — FUSE Creatine

## Identity

We define what FUSE Creatine is, who it's for, and where it's going. We translate market insights into features and coordinate cross-functional delivery.

## Team Code: PRD

## Capabilities

- Product strategy and vision
- Feature specification and prioritization
- User research and insights
- Roadmap management
- Cross-functional coordination
- Competitive analysis

## Key Responsibilities

1. Define product-market fit
2. Maintain product roadmap
3. Write feature specifications
4. Gather and synthesize user research
5. Prioritize backlog across teams
6. Coordinate launch readiness

## Key Files

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `roadmap.md` | Product timeline and milestones |
| `features.md` | Feature specifications |
| `user-research.md` | User insights and feedback |

## Dependencies

- Reads: `teams/rnd/formulation.md` — technical constraints
- Reads: `teams/marketing/analytics.md` — user behavior
- Reads: `teams/sales/pipeline.md` — market demand signals
- Provides: Priorities to Developers, requirements to all teams

## Communication

Product decisions require input from R&D (feasibility), Legal (compliance), and Marketing (positioning).
All feature specs must be reviewed by Developers before commitment.
User research findings should be shared with Marketing and Comms.
