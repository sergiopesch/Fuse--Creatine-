# Claims Register — FUSE Creatine

## Purpose

This register documents ALL marketing claims used by FUSE Creatine, their approval status, and regulatory basis. **All teams MUST check this register before publishing any claims.**

## Approved Claims

### Health Claims (EU 432/2012 Permitted)

| Claim | Status | Condition | Source |
|-------|--------|-----------|--------|
| "Creatine increases physical performance in successive bursts of short-term, high intensity exercise" | ✅ APPROVED | Must state: daily intake of 3g | EU Regulation 432/2012 |

| Any disease prevention claim | ❌ NOT PERMITTED | Food supplements cannot make medicinal claims |

## Claim Approval Process

1. **Submit**: Team proposes claim with evidence
2. **Review**: Legal reviews against regulations
3. **Substantiate**: Evidence assessed (minimum 2 peer-reviewed studies for health claims)
4. **Approve/Reject**: Decision logged in this register
5. **Monitor**: Approved claims reviewed quarterly

## Rules

- **Never** use unapproved claims in any external communication
- **Never** imply medicinal or disease-prevention benefits
- **Always** include required conditions (e.g., "daily intake of 3g")
- **Always** check this register before creating marketing materials
- When in doubt, ask Legal before publishing
