## Regulatory Context

FUSE is a **food supplement** containing creatine monohydrate. Key regulations:
- UK Food Supplements Regulations 2003
- EU Regulation 1924/2006 (nutrition and health claims)
- EU Regulation 432/2012 (permitted health claims)

## Permitted Claims (Creatine)

Per EU 432/2012, creatine can claim:
> "Creatine increases physical performance in successive bursts of short-term, high intensity exercise."

Conditions: Daily intake of 3g creatine.
