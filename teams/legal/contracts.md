## Contract Review Process

1. Draft or receive contract
2. Legal review (compliance, IP protection, liability)
3. CEO approval for financial commitments
4. Execute and file
5. Calendar review dates

## Key Contract Principles

- Always include NDA provisions for formulation details
- Ensure IP ownership is clearly assigned to FUSE
- Include quality standards and audit rights for suppliers
- Specify governing law (England and Wales)
- Include termination clauses with reasonable notice periods
