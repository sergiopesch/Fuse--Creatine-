1. **Identify** — Research and qualify prospects
2. **Outreach** — Initial contact with pitch
3. **Discovery** — Understand needs and fit
4. **Proposal** — Send partnership proposal
5. **Negotiate** — Terms and pricing
6. **Close** — Agreement signed
7. **Onboard** — Launch partnership

- Pricing must be CEO-approved before proposals
