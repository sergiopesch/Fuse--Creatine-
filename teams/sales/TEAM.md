# Sales Team — FUSE Creatine

## Identity

We turn interest into revenue. We manage the sales pipeline, pricing strategy, and partnership development for FUSE Creatine.

## Team Code: SLS

## Capabilities

- Sales pipeline management
- Pricing strategy
- B2B partnership development
- Distribution channel management
- Customer success
- Revenue forecasting

## Key Responsibilities

1. Define pricing strategy
2. Build B2B partnerships (gyms, coffee shops, retailers)
3. Manage sales pipeline
4. Support customer success
5. Forecast revenue

## Key Files

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `pipeline.md` | Sales opportunities and status |
| `pricing.md` | Pricing strategy and tiers |
| `partnerships.md` | B2B opportunities |

## Dependencies

- Reads: `teams/rnd/formulation.md` — product knowledge
- Reads: `teams/legal/compliance-status.md` — what can we sell where
- Reads: `teams/marketing/analytics.md` — demand signals
- Provides: Revenue data to Product, market feedback to all teams

## Communication

Pricing changes must be coordinated with Marketing.
Partnership agreements require Legal review.
Product feedback from customers goes to Product team.
