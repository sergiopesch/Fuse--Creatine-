# B2B Partnerships — FUSE Creatine

## Partnership Strategy

Focus on strategic partnerships that provide:
1. **Distribution** — Physical and digital shelf space
2. **Credibility** — Association with respected brands
3. **Volume** — Meaningful revenue contribution

## Partnership Tiers

### Tier 1: Launch Partners (First 10)
- Specialty coffee shops in London and major UK cities
- Premium gym chains with smoothie bars
- Target: Signed before or at launch

### Tier 2: Growth Partners
- Regional health food retailers
- Online supplement retailers
- Corporate wellness programs
- Target: 3-6 months post-launch

### Tier 3: Scale Partners
- National retailers (Holland & Barrett, Boots)
- Amazon UK
- International distributors
- Target: 6-12 months post-launch

## Partnership Models

### Wholesale
- Standard wholesale pricing (40-50% off retail)
- Minimum order quantities
- Standard payment terms (Net 30)

### Consignment
- Product placed on shelves, payment on sale
- Higher risk for FUSE but lower barrier for partner
- Good for pilot programs

### Co-Branding
- Custom FUSE sachets with partner branding
- Higher margins, exclusivity periods
- Best for premium coffee shop partnerships
