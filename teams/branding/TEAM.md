# Branding Team — FUSE Creatine

## Identity

We define how FUSE looks and feels. We own the visual identity, design system, packaging, and UI/UX across all touchpoints.

## Team Code: BRD

## Capabilities

- Visual identity design
- Design system management
- Product packaging design
- UI/UX design
- Brand guidelines
- Asset creation

## Key Responsibilities

1. Maintain brand visual identity
2. Design product packaging
3. Create and manage design system
4. Ensure visual consistency across touchpoints
5. Support Marketing and Digital Content with assets

## Brand Essentials

**Logo**: "FUSE CREATINE" — "FUSE" in brand red (#ff3b30), "CREATINE" in white
**Tagline**: "Britain's First Coffee-Optimised Creatine"
**Personality**: Scientific, premium, British, confident
