# Packaging Design — FUSE Creatine

## Status: Concept Phase

Packaging design has not started. Waiting on R&D to confirm product dimensions and formulation.

## Product Format

**Primary**: Individual sachets (single-serve)
**Secondary**: Box containing 30 sachets (1 month supply)

## Sachet Requirements

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| Contents | 3g or 5g creatine monohydrate (TBD) |
| Material | TBD (food-grade, recyclable preferred) |
| Dimensions | TBD (depends on formulation) |
| Opening | Easy-tear |
| Moisture barrier | Required (creatine is hygroscopic) |

## Box Requirements

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| Contents | 30 sachets |
| Material | Cardboard, premium feel |
| Finish | Matte with spot UV on logo (concept) |
| Sustainability | Recyclable, minimal plastic |

## Label Requirements (Legal)

Required information on packaging:
- Product name and description
- Ingredients list
- Nutritional information per serving
- Net weight
- Batch number and expiry date
- Storage instructions
- Manufacturer details
- Health claims (only EU 432/2012 approved)
- Allergen information
- Barcode (EAN-13)

See `teams/legal/compliance-status.md` for regulatory requirements.

## Design Direction

- Premium, minimal aesthetic
- Dark background with FUSE Red accent
- Clear product hierarchy: Logo → Product name → Key benefit
- "Made in Britain" badge
- GMP Certified badge
- Science-forward (not gym-bro aesthetic)

## Dependencies

- R&D: Product dimensions and format confirmation
- Legal: Label compliance requirements
- Sales: Retailer packaging requirements (if B2B)
