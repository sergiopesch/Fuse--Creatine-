## Voice Guidelines

### Tone Attributes
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Scientific** but not clinical
- **Simple** but not simplistic
- **Warm** but not soft
- **Premium** but not pretentious
- **British** but not stuffy

### Do Say
- "Zero friction"
- "One habit"
- "Watch it disappear"
- "Already doing this"
- "Pour and go"
- "Science, simplified"
- "Quiet improvement"
- "Your coffee, upgraded"

### Don't Say
- "Hack your biology"
- "10x your morning"
- "Crush your goals"
- "Beast mode"
- "Revolutionary breakthrough"
- "Game-changer"
- "Unlike anything before"
- "Disruptive innovation"

### Word Choices

| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|
| Revolutionary | Elegant |
| Groundbreaking | Simple |
| Cutting-edge | Thoughtful |
| Optimize | Improve |
| Biohack | Upgrade |
| Supplement | Addition |
| Routine | Habit |
| Synergy | Fit |

## Narrative Pillars

### Pillar 1: Radical Simplicity
> "The best health decision is the one you don't have to make."

- Attach to existing habits
- Remove steps, don't add them
- Complexity is the enemy
- Friction kills consistency

**Content themes:** Before/after routines, simplicity comparisons, "one habit" messaging

### Pillar 2: Biological Precision
> "We don't invent science. We deliver it better."

- Evidence-first approach
- Creatine is proven—we just re-packaged it
- Respect for existing research
- Transparent about what we know

**Content themes:** Study citations, science explainers, "500+ studies" references

### Pillar 3: Human Elevation
> "Small improvements, compounded daily, become extraordinary."

- Not about peaks—about baselines
- Quiet improvement > loud transformation
- Health is a practice, not an event
- Accessible to everyone

**Content themes:** Long-term thinking, compound effects, "every morning" consistency

### Pillar 4: Transparency
> "We tell you exactly what's in it and why."

- No proprietary blends
- No hiding behind marketing
- Open about what we know and don't know
- Build trust through honesty

**Content themes:** Ingredient breakdowns, "no BS" positioning, honest FAQ

## Objection Responses (In-Voice)

### "Isn't this just a gimmick?"
> "Creatine is the most proven supplement on Earth. 500+ studies. ISSN-recommended. The only gimmick would be adding friction to take it. We removed all of it."

### "Coffee and creatine don't mix"
> "Actually, they do. Creatine is stable in coffee temperatures for 10+ minutes—far longer than you'll take to drink it. We've tested this. The science holds."

### "I already take creatine"
> "Great. How's your consistency? If it's 100%, you don't need us. If it's not—this might be why."

### "Why creatine specifically?"
> "It's the starting point, not the finish line. Creatine is the most researched, most proven, most widely beneficial compound we could start with. More is coming."

### "This seems expensive"
> "Compare it to what you already spend on coffee. One premium latte = £4. FUSE per serving = £X. You're already investing in your morning. This makes it smarter."
