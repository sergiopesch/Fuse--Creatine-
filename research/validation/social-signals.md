## Sentiment Analysis

### Positive Signals (Look For)
- "I'd definitely try this"
- "Finally someone thought of this"
- "Where can I get it?"
- "This is genius"
- "Take my money"
- Questions about pricing/availability

### Neutral Signals
- "Interesting concept"
- "Never thought about it"
- "Would need to see it work"
- Questions about science/safety

### Negative Signals (Learn From)
- "Just take pills"
- "Sounds gimmicky"
- "Why would you ruin coffee"
- Concerns about taste
- Skepticism about claims
