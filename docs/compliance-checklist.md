# Compliance Checklist — FUSE Creatine

## Purpose

This checklist ensures all FUSE activities meet UK/EU regulatory requirements. Every team should reference this before launching external-facing work.

## Product Compliance

- [ ] Product classified as food supplement (not medicinal)
- [ ] Creatine monohydrate on EU positive list (confirmed)
- [ ] Novel Foods assessment not required (confirmed)
- [ ] GMP-certified manufacturer selected
- [ ] HACCP compliance at manufacturing facility
- [ ] Batch testing protocol established
- [ ] Heavy metals testing included
- [ ] Microbiological testing included

## Labelling Compliance (UK Food Supplements Regulations 2003)

- [ ] Product name and description
- [ ] "Food supplement" designation
- [ ] Complete ingredients list
- [ ] Nutritional information per serving
- [ ] Recommended daily intake
- [ ] "Do not exceed stated recommended daily dose"
- [ ] "Food supplements should not be used as a substitute for a varied and balanced diet and a healthy lifestyle"
- [ ] "Keep out of the reach of young children"
- [ ] Batch number
- [ ] Best before date
- [ ] Net quantity
- [ ] Manufacturer name and address
- [ ] Storage conditions
- [ ] Country of origin
- [ ] Allergen information
- [ ] Barcode (EAN-13)

## Health Claims Compliance (EU 1924/2006 & 432/2012)

- [ ] Only EU 432/2012 permitted claims used
- [ ] Required conditions stated (e.g., "daily intake of 3g creatine")
- [ ] No implied disease prevention or treatment
- [ ] No exaggerated or misleading claims
- [ ] Claims register maintained and updated (`teams/legal/claims-register.md`)
- [ ] All marketing materials reviewed by Legal before publication

## IP Compliance

- [ ] Freedom-to-Operate analysis completed
- [ ] Trademark search completed for "FUSE"
- [ ] No infringement on existing patents
- [ ] Trade secrets protected (NDA with suppliers)

## Digital Compliance (GDPR)

- [ ] Privacy policy published (✅)
- [ ] Terms of service published (✅)
- [ ] Cookie consent mechanism live (✅)
- [ ] Data processing agreements with third parties
- [ ] Right to erasure process defined
- [ ] Data breach notification process defined

## Advertising Compliance (ASA/CAP Code)

- [ ] All advertising claims substantiated
- [ ] No misleading comparisons
- [ ] Price claims accurate
- [ ] "First" claims verifiable
- [ ] Social media posts comply with #ad requirements (if applicable)
